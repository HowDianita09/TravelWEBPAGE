Travel to Maldives!

The Maldives is a paradise destination known for its crystal clear waters, white sand beaches, and incredible marine biodiversity. They are located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India, and consist of approximately 1,200 islands.

List of activities to do in the Maldives

  1. Diving and snorkeling: The clear waters and colorful coral reefs make the Maldives a paradise for diving and snorkeling.
  2. Water sports: You can practice kayaking, paddleboarding, surfing, and kitesurfing.
  3. Boat excursions: You can take cruises to see dolphins or spectacular sunsets, as well as tours to other islands.

Accommodation

  • Overwater Resorts: The Maldives is famous for its overwater bungalows or villas, which offer an incredible level of luxury and privacy. They are designed to blend in with the natural surroundings and many have private pools.

Gastronomy

Maldivian cuisine is mainly based on fish, especially tuna, and coconut. Some traditional dishes are "garudhiya" (fish soup) and "mas huni" (mixture of shredded tuna with coconut and onion). There is also influence from Indian and Asian cuisine.
